About Us

History

  • 1999

    Our Humble Beginnings

    SNDT Tulay Para sa Kabataan Foundation started in year 1999 in Quezon City, in Congressional Subdivision, Project 6. From then on, the foundation has set its objective to establish a parish-based scholarship program that will assist the needy but deserving students to develop their potential through education, likewise providing a channel / opportunity for parishioners to help by sharing their resources. With active parishioners lending a hand, the foundation desires not just to provide financial aid but aims to foster a well-rounded growth of the youth by likewise helping in the emotional and spiritual development of the scholar.

  • 2013

    A Growing Foundation

    From merely seven scholars in 1999, the scholars continued to increase in numbers as more and more sponsors had been willing to provide assistance. Currently, the foundation already has 22 College and 11 High School Active Scholars.

  • 2019

    Continued Bridging

    Today, the SNDT Tulay Para sa Kabataan Educational Foundation continues to help bridge the gap between poverty and education making more hopes and dreams of the youth realized.

Scholarship

Highschool

The High School Scholarship program of Tulay Para sa Kabataan Scholarship Foundation are open yearly to those deserving and needy students who wish to pursue their secondary education.

  • Qualifications
  1. Must be a Filipino citizen
  2. Must be an incoming student (any level) in any Public High School in Quezon City
  3. Must be financially needy
  4. Must have good moral character
  5. Must have a final general weighted average (GWA) of at least 82% in the last school year attended
  • Requirements
    1. Photocopy of Report Card (Form 138) of the latest school year attended
    2. Photocopy of Certificate of Good Moral Character obtained from the latest school attended
    3. Photocopy of Birth Certificate
    4. One copy of recent 1×1 picture

    *Original copies must be presented for verification

  • Benefits
    1. Initial Allowance (for school supplies, books, etc.)
    2. Allowance (Weekly)
    3. Scholastic Excellence Award (Quarterly)
    4. Free Tutorial

    *Graduating scholar/s is/are entitled of Php 500 subsidy for College Entrance Examination Fee.
